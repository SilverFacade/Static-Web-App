The Baker's Dozen

Here at the Baker's Dozen we have the Baker's Ranch directly in the backyard. This Ranch has been part of the Baker's Family for many generations, and we are here to ensure that it gets put to good use and respects the wishes of the original owners. Furthermore, we are against using any kind of chemicals or genetically modified crops here at the Baker's Ranch to ensure our customers are receiving the highest quality of pies.
