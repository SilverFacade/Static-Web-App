The Baker's Dozen

Welcome to our home page! We are pleased to have you here and hope you enjoy everything you see. Our company cares deeply for our customers day in and day out. There is nothing that pleases us more than serving you. Here at the Baker's Dozen, we like to bake pies completely from scratch using our crafted homemade recipes and ingredients directly from our backyard! This is the secret to how we keep everything fresh just for you all the time.

On the left here, we have displayed a freshly baked apple pie. It has the perfect amount of cinnamon and will blow your taste buds away! The apples come freshly picked from the Baker's Ranch, and they are always used the day that they are picked from the trees. This pie was our best-seller for all of 2023, and we hope that you enjoy it as much as the next person!

Not a fan of apples or cinnamon? That's quite alright as we have this freshly baked cherry pie on the right as well. Its tartness is balanced with a sweet, flaky crust making it the perfect pie for any occasion!
